Depths and Dangers

40 years ago to this day the SS liberal went down outside of long island while it was chartering a group of fisherman. Everyone was able to be saved but the boat was completely lost with a lot of valuables left inside they have never been recovered. A group of expert scuba divers that were good friends that decided it what be a good idea to try to recover anything from the shipwreck. After arriving at the boat rental place they are offered different types of boats they could use for the day. They decide on a boat and set off for their day. The day is beautiful with absolutely calm water. Two hours later they arrive at the wreck around 50 miles offshore. The depth of the ocean is surprisingly low here so they all put on their gear and hop into the water like any other day.
